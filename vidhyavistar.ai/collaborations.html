Collaborations

At Vidhya Vistar, collaboration is more than a partnership. it's a strategic alliance that shapes the future of education. Through SKYGET, we work closely with government bodies, educational institutions, investors, and NGOs to deliver scalable, AI-powered learning systems.

Government & Policy-Level Engagements

SKYGET has aligned with state education departments to pilot and scale AI-based learning in compliance with national frameworks like NEP 2020, Nadu-Nedu, and ICT Education Policies. Our platform is structured to meet the policy goals of states like Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Karnataka, Maharashtra, and Tamil Nadu.

Institutional Alliances

We partner with schools, colleges, and universities to deploy SKYGET's AI modules tailored to regional and curriculum-specific needs. Our focus includes curriculum mapping, performance tracking, administrative automation, and personalized learning all backed by institutional training programs.

Industry & CSR Engagements

With the support of private sector leaders and CSR foundations, we scale our AI capabilities to underserved schools and remote learning clusters. These partnerships allow us to deliver cost-effective infrastructure, multilingual learning access, and regional deployment at scale.

Training & Accreditation Collaboration

We work with teacher training institutes, skill development boards, and certification councils to offer AI-integration workshops and digital teaching certifications. This ensures that educators are not only tech-ready, but also empowered to unlock SKYGET's full potential in the classroom.
