About Us

Vidhya Vistar is a premier education technology management company committed to revitalizing and scaling transformative learning solutions. As the strategic backbone behind SKYGET, we integrate vision, innovation, and execution to redefine how institutions, educators, and learners experience education.

What is SKYGET?

What is SKYGET? SKYGET is an advanced AI-powered EdTech platform designed to transform education through intelligent automation and personalized learning experiences. It equips schools, students, and administrators with cutting-edge tools for AI-driven engagement, adaptive assessments, regulatory compliance, and scalable digital infrastructure.

By bridging traditional learning models with future-ready methodologies, SKYGET is purpose-built to align with national and state-level educational reforms—empowering institutions to evolve with confidence and impact.

SKYGET’s Key Achievements

  • AI-Powered Innovation: Enables personalized learning journeys, automates institutional workflows, and delivers real-time insights to all stakeholders.
  • Government Collaborations: Partnered with state-level institutions, contributing to national education missions with scalable EdTech solutions.
  • EdTech Recognition: Recognized for addressing systemic educational challenges with intelligent, adaptive technologies.
  • Competitive Exams Module:Offers dedicated modules for JEE, NEET, and Intermediate preparation powered by AI-driven guidance and evaluation.
  • Strategic Partnerships: Strong alliances with educational institutions, investors, and government bodies to drive widespread adoption.
  • Performance Monitoring: Provides robust tools for educators, parents, and institutions to track student performance and foster continuous improvement.
